What is Igoan ?

Igoan is a web based directory of free (as in free software) projects. Its four main ideas are:

  • Unlimited access, without any ads, popup, login screen, whatever.
  • Only Free Software projects. Just opensource is not enough.
  • No proprietary software involved for the website.
  • The site itself is also free software: you can browse the cvs to see the source, re-distribute it under the same licence, get involved in the developpement, etc.
